Frequency Game

  1. Appoint scorekeeper for the game.
  2. Each team gets 7 minutes per turn
  3. Appoint a cluemaster for your team
  4. Cluemaster picks a spectrum and clicks reveal to see secret frequency
  5. Cluemaster types 5-word phrase based on spectrum/frequency
  6. Cluemaster then screen shares and their team guesses frequency value
  7. Opposing team guesses if actual value is higher or lower.
  8. Click submit to see who got points.
  9. Jot down the score. It's now the other team's turn
  10. First team to get 10 points wins

Note: Refreshing page will reset timer, frequency value, and spectrums.
